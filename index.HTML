Facts About Ted

Ted was originally named Pocus while his brother was named Hocus... just like Hocus Pocus! When we adopted him, we named him Teddy because he resembled a teddy bear, however he eventually started being called Ted as we discovered he was a bit of a terror, and Ted the Terror seemed fitting. His favorite snack seems to be leaves he steals from our (non-toxic!) plants inside our home. His hobbies consist of ankle biting, sleeping, and harassing his older brother.

Why

Why did I make a page dedicated to Ted? Well Ted is a silly little guy with a big attitude. His stories are entertaining and he's cute, which makes him perfect for his very own website.
